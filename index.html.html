About COVID-19

What is COVID-19 (Corona Virus)

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVIDâ€‘19) is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). It was first identified in December 2019 in Wuhan, Hubei, China, and has resulted in an ongoing pandemic.

The source of the Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) has not been identified; however, most of the cases were epidemiologically linked to the seafood and animal market in Wuhan, China, as the initial spread happened amongst the shoppers who were present there. There are current studies taking place to determine the actual source of the disease.

COVID-19 Symptoms

6 Steps Prevention Against Coronavirus

Wash your hands regularlyfor 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze

Avoid close contact with people and maintain minimum 2 metre distance

Stay home and self-isloate from others in the household if you feel unwell

Avoid touching eyes, nose and mouth with unwashed hands.

Monitor yourself regularly for any symptoms or fever.
